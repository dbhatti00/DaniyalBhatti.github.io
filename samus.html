Description

This hard surface sculpt of Nintendos iconic character Samus Aran showcases my ability to create intricate, detailed forms with precision. Using ZBrush, I sculpted the iconic armor, paying close attention to its mechanical design and distinct shapes. The textures, crafted in Substance Painter, bring the piece to life with carefully applied metallic materials, subtle weathering, and vibrant accents. Then using PBR materials I brought the model to life in Maya. This model highlights my expertise in both hard surface sculpting and texturing, capturing the essence of the character while demonstrating my technical and artistic capabilities.
