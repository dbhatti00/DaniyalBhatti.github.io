Description

For this project, I created a detailed hard-surface sculpt of a mech/robot, focusing on intricate mechanical components and advanced detailing. Using ZBrush, I sculpted the body, joints, and armor plating with precision, ensuring each part was designed to reflect functionality and strength.

‎

The design features a combination of smooth, segmented surfaces and complex, mechanical details, like pistons and vents, to give the model a believable, industrial feel. I paid special attention to the flow and connectivity between parts, making sure that the movement of the mech’s limbs would look natural and logical. After sculpting, I applied basic hard-surface texturing in Substance Painter to bring out the metallic surfaces, wear-and-tear, and surface details. This project allowed me to refine my hard-surface sculpting skills while bringing a dynamic mechanical character to life.
