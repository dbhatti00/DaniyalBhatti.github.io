Description

Inspired by the horse companion in Metal Gear Solid 5, I sculpted a detailed 3D model of a horse in ZBrush, focusing on anatomical accuracy and realistic features like muscles, mane, and hooves. Afterward, I textured it in Substance Painter, adding lifelike details. I then posed the horse to capture a dynamic energy. This project allowed me to combine my love for the game with my skills in sculpting and texturing to create a realistic and dynamic horse model.
