Description

This is a realistic character sculpted in ZBrush, where I focused on accurate anatomical proportions and intricate surface details to enhance realism. After sculpting, I performed efficient retopology in Maya, ensuring clean topology for animation and rendering. For texturing, I used Substance Painter to create lifelike skin, baking high-resolution maps to capture the sculpted details. I generated realistic hair using XGen in Maya, carefully grooming and styling it to match natural hair flow.
