Process

This project was a great opportunity to first dive into Substance Painter and refine my 3D skills. I sculpted a semi-realistic hand in ZBrush, focusing on muscle definition and skin details. Using Substance Painter, I applied realistic textures, and baked a normal map onto the model from a high poly mesh. I then rigged and posed the hand in Blender, ensuring flexibility for a natural pose. Finally, I rendered the scene in Maya, where I fine-tuned lighting and materials for a polished look. This project helped me strengthen my abilities in sculpting, texturing, and rigging within the 3D pipeline.
