Description

This ZBrush sculpt showcases a witch character with intricate detailing, emphasizing craftsmanship and design. The character is presented in a neutral pose with carefully refined anatomy, balanced proportions, and realistic fabric folds in her outfit. Subtle elements, such as her hat, accessories, and garments, reflect a magical theme while allowing room for further adaptation into animation or game-ready models. This project highlights my ability to create detailed and polished character sculpts with clean topology and attention to design aesthetics.
