Description

Inspired by the stunning visual style of Arcane, I created a stylized sculpt of the character Jinx that emphasizes painterly textures and bold, exaggerated details. Sculpted in ZBrush, I focused on capturing the show's unique aesthetic through strong shapes and refined proportions. I developed the textures with a hand-painted approach in Substance Painter, layering color and detail to evoke a vivid, illustrated quality that mirrors Arcane's artistry. Finally, I rendered the model in Maya to bring out the full depth and vibrancy of the textures, giving the piece an almost animated feel that honors the captivating look of the show.
