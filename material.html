Description

Constructed entirely within Substance Designer, this 100% procedural PBR material achieves realistic ground surface complexity through a layering process. I began by defining the primary rock structure using procedural noises and functions to sculpt the heightmap, capturing sharp and rounded forms. Water elements were then integrated using a mask derived directly from the rock's height data, ensuring puddles naturally occupy the lowest areas. Finally, greenery and moss were procedurally blended onto the surface based on factors like slope and ambient occlusion, creating an organic, natural distribution.
