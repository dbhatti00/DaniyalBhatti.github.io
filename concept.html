Description

Inspired by the original concept art by Anton Medvedev on ArtStation, this stylized character was brought to life in 3D using a blend of organic and hard-surface modeling techniques. The design showcases a unique fusion of human anatomy and cybernetics, emphasizing personality and dynamic storytelling through vibrant textures and bold shapes.

‎

The fully rigged model makes use of Zbrush projection and normal maps to ensure both detail and performance.
