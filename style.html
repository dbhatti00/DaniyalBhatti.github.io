Description

Based off a charcter design by Danii Suponitskiy, this stylized 3D character embodies a mix of grit and sophistication. Designed with a painterly aesthetic, the character features sharp facial expressions, a detailed suit, and subtle storytelling elements, like the cigarette in hand, to enhance the noir-inspired mood. The texturing and attention to anatomy blend seamlessly with the stylized shading, showcasing a balance between realism and artistry. Perfect for animation or game-ready assets.
