Process

Inspired by the character Tulio from The Road to El Dorado, I set out to create a stylized bust that captured his expressive, adventurous personality. In ZBrush, I focused on defining exaggerated features like the strong jawline and playful smirk, which are key to Tulio’s charm. I used the Boolean tool to create a “mouth box,” carving out space inside the mouth to help achieve a more flexible, animated look. This technique added depth to the expression and gave me greater control for refining the shape. The project was a fun blend of stylization and technical sculpting, letting me capture the animated feel of a beloved character in 3D form.
