Description

This chair model highlights a hybrid workflow. The top section has been retopologized for optimized geometry, while the bottom retains its high-poly sculpt to preserve intricate detail. I baked the high-poly details into a normal map and used Substance Painter to create detailed, realistic textures. This approach ensures visual fidelity while maintaining an efficient geometry setup, demonstrating my adaptability and expertise in combining sculpting, retopology, and texturing techniques for a polished final asset.
