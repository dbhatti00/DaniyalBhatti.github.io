Process

For this freelance project with PlayEnvy, I sculpted and textured multiple detailed 3D models of child for a playground design. I focused on creating realistic skin by layering textures for pores, veins, and skin imperfections using Substance Painter. The sculpting process in ZBrush involved refining facial features and anatomical details, ensuring a lifelike appearance.

‎

In addition to the skin, I also created the clothing textures in Substance Painter, making sure they felt natural and integrated seamlessly with the character. The model’s skin was textured with subsurface scattering to simulate realistic light interaction, and veins were subtly placed beneath the surface for added depth. After rigging and posing the model in Blender, I rendered the scene in Maya, capturing the child in a playful, dynamic pose. This project helped refine my skills in sculpting, texturing, and rendering for realistic character design.
